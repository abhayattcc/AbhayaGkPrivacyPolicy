Privacy Policy — AbhayaGK

Effective date: June 17, 2025  |  Last updated: June 17, 2025

AbhayaGK is a free General Knowledge quiz and reference app developed for educational purposes. This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) is collected when you use this app, and how it is handled. By using AbhayaGK, you agree to the practices described here.
01

Information We Do Not Collect

AbhayaGK is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Contacts, camera, or microphone data
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Financial or payment information

We do not require you to create an account or sign in to use the app.

02

How the App Works

AbhayaGK operates primarily as a WebView-based application. The app loads educational content (General Knowledge topics, quizzes, and reference material) from a hosted web source. This is similar to how a browser works — the content is displayed within the app.

The app does not run any background services, does not track your activity, and does not communicate with any external server for data collection purposes.

03

Internet Permission

The app requires the INTERNET permission in order to load web content for the GK quiz and reference sections. This connection is used solely to display educational content within the app's WebView. No personal data is sent over this connection.

04

Third-Party Services

AbhayaGK does not currently integrate any of the following third-party services:

  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network)
  • Crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics)
  • Social login providers (e.g., Google, Facebook)

If third-party services are added in a future version, this policy will be updated before the update is published.

05

Children's Privacy

AbhayaGK is an educational General Knowledge app suitable for all ages, including children. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country).

Since no personal data is collected at all, the app is safe for use by children with parental guidance.

06

Data Security

Because AbhayaGK does not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data to protect or risk of a data breach involving your personal information.

The app stores no user data locally beyond standard Android system caches, which are automatically managed by the operating system.

07

Links to External Content

The app may display web content from an external source. If that content contains links to third-party websites, please note that we have no control over those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
